Hi! My name is Tutti.

  • Domestic Shorthair
  • Spayed Female
  • 8.5 years old
  • 16 pounds

If you've ever experienced being picked last at a team draft or a dance, you know how I feel. For some reason, prospective adopters have passed me by without getting to know me and what a great pet I could be if given a chance. The staff and volunteers know how special I am and hope that you consider me when you're looking for a new pet. Some of the activities I enjoy include: Lounging, getting scratched by the ears, laying in the sun. Would you like to meet me?

Hi! My name is Dougie.

  • Mini Rex / Rabbit
  • Neutered Male
  • 4 years old
  • 5 pounds

I was in a shelter that lacked the resources to continue to care for me, so I came to Animal Humane Society to find a new home.

I am a Shy Snuggler! Shy Snugglers are relaxed, gentle and a little bit timid. I'm affectionate and enjoy human interaction, but also need time alone. I'm rarely mischievous and often live harmoniously with other pets. I would enjoy a calm, quiet home and am a great choice for first-time adopters and homes with gentle children of all ages. Would you like to meet me?
